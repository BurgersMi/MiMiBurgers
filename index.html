"MiMiBurgers" Best Burgers in Miami!

We only use fresh ingredients
Now there are 13 types of burgers on the menu - with chicken, turkey, beef, pork and various fillers. You can order a standard size burger or "Big" if you're really hungry.

About Company

You can also order "Lucky set", which includes three types of burgers, potatoes, sauces, chicken nuggets and grilled corn.

"MiMiBurgers" is the first burger restaurant located in Miami itself, which has dedicated itself exclusively to one dish. Here, with a burger, you will be served French fries, salad, sauces and unlimited Repsi.

The feature of "MiMiBurgers"

The feature of "MiMiBurgers" is an open kitchen equipped with a huge Argentinean grill. So you can place an order and watch how the chefs work and prepare it for you. In addition to a significant assortment of burgers, the institution is famous for its American steaks, friendly atmosphere and one of the best cocktail menus in the city.
